menu_book Ancient Lore

Deep within the fractured realms of the Aether, beyond the reach of the High Council's light, entities older than recorded time dwell in silence. The Eldritch Weaver is not merely a beast, but a manifestation of the Void's desire to consume order. It spins threads of pure nothingness, catching the unwary in webs that drain not just physical strength, but memories and mana alike. Those who have witnessed it speak of a chilling silence preceding its arrival, a sudden dimming of starlight, and the scent of ozone. To bind such a creature to one's grimoire requires a sacrifice of immense arcane power, and a willful submission to the whispering madness that accompanies its presence.
